Types of Restaurants & How to Order

Fast Food / Casual Chains

Examples: KFC, McDonald's, Ajisen Ramen, Yoshinoya

  • Usually have picture menus
  • Order at counter
  • Often have English or number ordering

Sit-Down Restaurants

  • Waiter will bring menu
  • Point to items you want
  • They'll confirm with you
  • Pay at cashier after eating (or sometimes at table)

Hot Pot (ç«é”…)

  • Choose soup base (spicy, non-spicy, or split pot)
  • Order ingredients from menu
  • Cook items yourself in the pot
  • Usually all-you-can-eat or pay-by-item

Street Food

  • Point at what you want
  • Use fingers to show quantity
  • Pay with cash or scan QR code
  • Great for trying local specialties!

Payment Tips

  1. Mobile payment: Scan QR code with Alipay or WeChat Pay
  2. Cash: Always accepted, but less common
  3. Credit cards: Major chains accept, small places usually don't
  4. Tipping: NOT expected in China!

What to Expect

  • Shared plates: Chinese meals are family-style, everyone shares
  • Hot water or tea: Usually free
  • Wet towels: For cleaning hands before eating
  • No napkins: Use tissues (bring your own)
  • Bones on table: It's normal to leave bones/shells on table
